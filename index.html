Zechuan Zhang (张泽川)

I am currently a PhD student at Zhejiang University in Hang Zhou, China. My PhD supervisor is Prof. Yi Yang. My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision, machine learning. I am particularly interested in 3D vision, 3D human reconstruction and neural rendering.

Prior to that, I obtained the B.Sc Degree in Geographical Information Science from Zhejiang University in 2023. I was also a member of Advanced Honor Class of Engineering Education (ACEE) at Chu Kochen Honors College (CKC) of Zhejiang University.
